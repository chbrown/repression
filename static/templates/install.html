Repression

PNAS recently published a paper by Adam Kramer et al revealing that back in January 2012, Facebook made its users happier or sadder for a week. On purpose! How dare they? The uproar on the interwebs is deafening.

You're probably like me. When I want to be manipulated, I will ask to be manipulated1, dammit!

So you want to be manipulated...

All you need is a Facebook account and Chrome!

When you download the extension above and log on to Facebook, the system will flip a coin, sentencing you to be happy or sad for a weekâ€”the duration of the experiment. On {{expires | date:"longDate"}}, we will email a survey to you at your username@facebook.com email address, which will ask you to judge whether you have been happier or sadder than usual. After answering, we will reveal which side of the coin you actually got, and show you all the posts that you missed on Facebook (at least the text part of these posts).
